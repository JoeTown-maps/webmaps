St Joe Missouri is an amazing place to live, work, or visit, with many historic neighborhoods and buildings. It is also the largest city in the St. Joseph Metropolitan Statistical Area (or MSA). The St Joseph MSA is a powerhouse in manufacturing (the 3rd largest industrial center in Missouri), in transportation and warehousing, and in healthcare.

This site will explore and explain St Joseph through maps (mostly) and allow everyone to see the good things going on as well as some problem areas that need attention.
